Hello there! I'm Bella Ratmelia

I am a Data Services Librarian with the Research and Data Services unit at Singapore Management University (SMU) Libraries.

My main responsibility is to support the faculty and students in leveraging data analysis methods to fulfil their teaching, research and learning needs. I love to read, play games, and learning new things in my free time. Current favourite readings: Blue Period by Tsubasa Yamaguchi and The Mysterious Affair at Styles by Agatha Christie.
